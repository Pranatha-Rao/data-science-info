The dataset contains stock prices of Applied Diarrheal Disease Research centre.

Column 1 refers to date
Column 2 (open) refers to open price i.e the price at which the stock was opened.
Column 3 (high) refers to the highest price of the stock at that date
Column 3 (low) refers to the lowest price of the stock 
Column 4 (close) refers to the closing price of the stock
Column 5 (volume) refers to the quantity of the stock.
