b) Probability Distribution for each F1 driver to win both the Singaporean and Russian Grand Prix
c) Probability for Mercedes to win both races
d) Probability for Mercedes to win at least one race
e) If Mercedes wins the first race, what is the probability that Mercedes wins the next one
f) If Mercedes wins at least one of these two races, what is the probability Mercedes wins both races
g) Similar to f) and g) for Ferrari, Alpha Romeo and McLaren
h)  Probability Mercedes wins both races, assuming races can be held on either rainy, sunny, cloudy, snowy or foggy days
